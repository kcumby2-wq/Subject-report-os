# Reporting — Weekly and Monthly Report Formats

Defines the structure for recurring reports saved to `output/reports/`.

## Weekly Report (Monday planning + Friday review)

- Goals pulse: one line per active goal — did it move, is it blocked
- Transcript volume and 48hr SLA hit rate for the week
- New Prospect Membership signups / churn
- Course Portal progression notes
- Active Profile pipeline movement
- Subject Media upsell performance (sent / clicked / converted)
- Open items waiting on Kyron

## Monthly Report (report generation + goal review)

- Full goal-by-goal status against Q3 2026 targets in `goals/goals.md`
- MRR and churn detail for Prospect Membership
- Completion rate detail for Course Portal
- Active Profile count vs. target (15 live profiles, consent forms on file)
- SM cross-sell conversion rate vs. 20% target
- SEO funnel: signups → membership conversion rate
- Recommended adjustments for next month
- Any goals ready to move to `goals/archive/` with outcome notes

## Voice

All reports follow `skills/srvoice/SKILL.md` — calm, specific, verified, no hype.
